Repair Science

Why Asphalt Repair is Essential Before Seal Coating

Proper repair work is the foundation of successful seal coating. Skipping essential repairs leads to premature failure and wasted investment.

The Foundation of Long-Lasting Protection

Applying seal coating over damaged asphalt is like painting over rust‚Äîit may look good initially, but the underlying problems will quickly resurface. Professional asphalt repair addresses structural issues that could compromise your entire seal coating investment.

Surface irregularities prevent proper sealer application and create weak spots where water can penetrate. Oil stains reduce adhesion, causing the sealer to peel and fail prematurely. Edge damage allows water infiltration that can undermine the entire pavement structure.

Our systematic repair approach ensures every surface issue is addressed before seal coating application. This comprehensive preparation extends seal coating life by 50-100% compared to sealing over unrepaired damage, providing dramatically better ROI on your pavement protection investment.
