Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch from coal tar to acrylic sealer?

Yes, but proper surface preparation is crucial. The previous coal tar must be well-adhered, and a compatibility primer may be needed. Most professionals recommend acrylic for reapplications in Texas.

Which sealer provides better curb appeal?

Both provide rich black appearance initially. Acrylic maintains color longer in Texas sun, while coal tar may fade and gray faster due to UV degradation.

Do I need different maintenance for each type?

Acrylic requires minimal maintenance beyond regular cleaning. Coal tar may need more frequent touch-ups, especially in high-traffic areas or after extreme heat periods.
