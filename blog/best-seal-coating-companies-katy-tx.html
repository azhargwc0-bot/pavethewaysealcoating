Red Flags: Companies to Avoid

Warning Signs of Poor Contractors

Avoid companies that exhibit these red flags, which indicate unprofessional practices or potential scams.

Immediate Red Flags:
  • Door-to-door solicitation: "We were working nearby..."
  • No written contracts: Verbal agreements only
  • Demand full payment upfront: Legitimate companies don't do this
  • No local address: Out-of-state companies with no local presence
  • Extremely low bids: Too good to be true pricing
  • High-pressure tactics: "Today only" special deals
Professional Red Flags:
  • No insurance verification: Can't provide certificates
  • Poor communication: Unreliable or unprofessional contact
  • No references available: Can't provide local customer contacts
  • Generic estimates: No specific measurements or details
  • Cash-only payments: No credit cards or checks accepted
  • No warranty offered: Won't guarantee their work
Remember: If something feels wrong or too good to be true, trust your instincts and get second opinions from reputable local companies.
