Professional Application Considerations

Material Success Depends on Application

Even the best materials fail without proper application techniques and professional expertise.

Critical Application Factors:
  • Surface preparation: Cleaning, repairs, proper adhesion
  • Weather conditions: Temperature, humidity, precipitation
  • Material handling: Proper mixing, temperature control
  • Application technique: Even coverage, proper thickness
  • Curing protection: Traffic control, weather protection
Quality Control Measures:
  • Material inspection: Verify quality before application
  • Coverage monitoring: Ensure adequate material thickness
  • Weather tracking: Optimal conditions throughout process
  • Curing verification: Proper cure before traffic exposure
  • Final inspection: Complete coverage, quality finish

Professional insight: The best material applied poorly will fail faster than a good material applied professionally. Choose quality materials AND experienced contractors.
