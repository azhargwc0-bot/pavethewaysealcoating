Regional Timing Considerations in Texas

Texas Regional Variations

Texas's size creates regional climate differences that affect optimal seal coating timing across the state.

South Texas (Houston, San Antonio, Austin)
  • Extended season: March through November
  • High humidity: Requires careful weather monitoring
  • Mild winters: December work occasionally possible
  • Early heat: Summer challenges start in May
North Texas (Dallas, Fort Worth)
  • Standard season: April through October
  • Freeze risk: No winter applications
  • Lower humidity: Better summer conditions
  • Temperature swings: More weather variability
East Texas (Tyler, Beaumont)
  • High rainfall: More weather delays
  • High humidity: Extended curing times
  • Spring storms: Careful timing required
  • Fall optimal: September-October best
West Texas (Midland, Lubbock)
  • Dry conditions: Lower humidity year-round
  • Temperature extremes: Hot summers, cold winters
  • Wind factors: High winds affect application
  • UV intensity: Rapid curing conditions
