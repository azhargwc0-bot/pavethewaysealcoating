Warning Signs: When to Act

Early intervention prevents more expensive problems and provides better restoration results.

Color Changes

Black asphalt turning gray or brown indicates UV damage

Surface Cracking

Hairline cracks appearing as asphalt becomes brittle

Aggregate Showing

Stones becoming visible through worn surface

Poor Water Beading

Water soaking in instead of beading up

Temperature Brittleness

Surface becoming hard and inflexible

Overall property appearance declining
