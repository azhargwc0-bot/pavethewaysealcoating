‚ùì Post-Storm Repair FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions
How do I assess storm damage to my Katy TX driveway?
Assess storm damage by documenting with photos, checking for new cracks, measuring water damage depth, inspecting drainage systems, and noting surface upheaval or debris impact damage. Wait for safe conditions and avoid hazardous areas during inspection.
Should I file an insurance claim for storm damage to my driveway?
File insurance claims for significant storm damage exceeding your deductible. Document thoroughly with photos, get professional assessment, and contact your insurance company within required timeframes. Most policies cover sudden weather-related damage.
Can I drive on my storm-damaged driveway?
Avoid driving on storm-damaged driveways until professional assessment. Visible cracks, standing water, surface upheaval, or base damage indicate structural compromise that could worsen with vehicle weight.
How quickly should I repair storm damage to my driveway?
Address storm damage immediately to prevent further deterioration. Make temporary repairs within 48-72 hours if possible, schedule permanent repairs within 2 weeks to avoid water infiltration and additional damage.
What emergency repairs can I do myself after a storm?
Safe DIY emergency repairs include debris removal, temporary crack sealing with store-bought materials, and basic drainage clearing. Leave structural repairs, electrical hazards, and major damage to professionals.
How long do storm damage repairs take in Katy TX?
Emergency repairs: 1-2 days. Minor permanent repairs: 3-5 days. Major reconstruction: 1-3 weeks. Timeline depends on damage extent, weather conditions, insurance process, and contractor availability.
