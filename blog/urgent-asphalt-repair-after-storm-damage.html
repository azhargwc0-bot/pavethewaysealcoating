🌊 Specific Storm Damage Types

Hurricane & Wind Storm Damage:

  • Debris Impact: Tree damage, flying object impacts, surface gouging
  • Water Damage: Flooding, prolonged saturation, base washout
  • Wind Uplift: Edge lifting, surface peeling, material displacement
  • Storm Surge: Saltwater infiltration, scouring, structural undermining

Hail Storm Damage:

  • Surface Impact: Pitting, aggregate exposure, texture damage
  • Crack Formation: Impact-induced cracking, stress concentration
  • Drainage Issues: Surface deformation affecting water flow
  • Protective Loss: Seal coating damage, accelerated aging

Flash Flood Damage:

  • Erosion Damage: Edge washout, surface scouring, material loss
  • Base Undermining: Foundation weakening, structural instability
  • Contamination: Debris infiltration, chemical contamination
  • Settlement: Uneven settling, surface depression, drainage problems
