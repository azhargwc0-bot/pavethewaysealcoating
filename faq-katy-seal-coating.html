What's the difference between coal tar and asphalt emulsion sealers?

+

Coal tar sealers offer superior resistance to gas, oil, and chemical spills, making them ideal for driveways with heavy vehicle use. Asphalt emulsion sealers are more environmentally friendly, dry faster, and provide excellent UV protection. Our experts will recommend the best sealer based on your specific needs and local climate conditions.
