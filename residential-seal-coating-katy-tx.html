Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I seal coat my driveway?

Most residential driveways should be seal coated every 2-3 years, depending on traffic, weather exposure, and the condition of the asphalt. High-traffic areas may need more frequent application.

What's the best time of year for seal coating?

Spring and fall are ideal for seal coating in Texas. We need temperatures between 50-85Â°F with no rain expected for 24 hours. Summer can work with early morning applications.

How long does the seal coating process take?

Most residential driveways can be completed in 4-6 hours. You can typically drive on the surface after 24 hours, with full cure achieved in 48-72 hours depending on weather conditions.

Do I need to be home during the service?

You don't need to be present during the entire process, but we do need access to water and coordination for vehicle movement. We'll discuss the schedule during your estimate.
