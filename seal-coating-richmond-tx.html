How often should I seal coat my Richmond driveway?

+

In Richmond's climate, driveways typically need seal coating every 2-3 years. Factors like sun exposure, traffic, and weather conditions can affect this timeline. Our team provides honest assessments of when your driveway actually needs service.

What's the best time of year for seal coating in Richmond, TX?

+

Late spring (April-May) and early fall (September-October) offer optimal conditions in Richmond. We avoid extreme summer heat and winter temperature fluctuations that can affect application quality and curing.

How long does seal coating take to dry in Richmond's humidity?

+

Typically 4-8 hours for foot traffic and 24-48 hours before driving, depending on temperature and humidity. Richmond's Gulf Coast influence can extend drying times, which we factor into our scheduling.

Do you service commercial properties in Richmond?

What warranty do you provide for Richmond seal coating work?

How much does seal coating cost in Richmond, TX?
